Why Forest and Landscape Restoration?

Forests are one of the most effective solutions to address the effects of climate change. They are also a powerful force to lift people out of poverty and conserve biodiversity.

Rather than just planting more trees, we work to restore landscapes and protect them from deforestation over the long term.

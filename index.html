Welcome to Nature Ideals

Ek aisi jagah jahan hum nature ko samajhte hain, uski protection karte hain aur ek behtar kal ka development karte hain. Humara maqsad hai puri duniya mein paryavaran ka gyan badhana.

Forests of the World

Amazon Rainforest (South America)

Trees: Rubber tree, Brazil nut tree, Mahogany.

Animals: Jaguar, Macaw, Poison Dart Frog, Sloth.

Rivers/Water: Amazon River.

Duniya ke "Lungs" kahe jane wale is jungle mein sabse zyada biodiversity hai.

Congo Basin (Africa)

Trees: Teak, Ebony, Moabi.

Animals: Gorillas, Chimpanzees, Forest Elephants.

Rivers/Water: Congo River.

Duniya ka dusra sabse bada tropical rainforest jo carbon absorb karne mein mahatvapurna hai.

Taiga Forest (North America & Eurasia)

Trees: Pine, Spruce, Larch (Coniferous trees).

Animals: Bears, Wolves, Moose, Siberian Tiger.

Rivers/Water: Numerous freshwater lakes and bogs.

Duniya ka sabse bada land biome jo barfili jagahon par fela hua hai.

Protection & Sustainable Development

Nature ko Kaise Bachayein?

  • Afforestation: Zaroorat se zyada ped lagayein aur kate hue jungalo ko wapas ugayein.
  • Reduce Carbon Footprint: Fossil fuels ka kam istemal karein, Solar/Wind energy ko badhava dein.
  • Wildlife Conservation: Janwaro ke shikar par rok aur unke natural habitat ko protect karna.
  • Plastic Ban: Oceans aur rivers ko bachane ke liye single-use plastic ka completely boycott.
  • Sustainable Development: Tarraki aise karein jisse nature ko nuksan na ho. Green buildings aur eco-friendly technology ka use karein.
